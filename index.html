Raw Honey

Nature’s Pure and Natural Sweetener

What is Raw Honey?

Raw honey is honey that is unprocessed, unpasteurized, and unheated. It is taken directly from the beehive, strained to remove debris, and bottled without losing its natural nutrients.

Benefits of Raw Honey

  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Supports digestion
  • Boosts immunity
  • Helps soothe sore throats
  • Natural energy source

Raw Honey vs Processed Honey

Unlike processed honey, raw honey retains its natural enzymes, vitamins, minerals, and pollen. Processed honey is often heated and filtered, which can remove many of these beneficial compounds.

Uses of Raw Honey

  • Sweetener for tea and coffee
  • Ingredient in cooking and baking
  • Natural skincare and face masks
  • Home remedies for coughs and colds

Storage Tips

Store raw honey in a cool, dry place at room temperature. Crystallization is natural and does not mean the honey has gone bad.
